Understanding, Simulating, and Supporting Visual Snow Syndrome

💠 What Is Visual Snow?

Visual Snow Syndrome (VSS) is a neurological condition that causes a static-like overlay in the visual field, among other symptoms. It can deeply affect quality of life and currently has no definitive cure.

🎮 Experience It

Try Our Visual Snow Simulator and adjust depth-of-field sliders to get a feel for how VSS is perceived.
